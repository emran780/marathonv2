About Oman Marathon Day

Oman Marathon Day is an exciting event that brings together runners of all ages and abilities to experience the thrill of marathon running in the beautiful city of Muscat.

Our event features three main types of runs:

  1. Kids' RUN: A fun and interactive run designed for children.
  2. 5KM RUN: A moderate-distance run suitable for both beginners and intermediate runners.
  3. 10KM RUN: A challenging run for experienced and competitive runners.

Join us and be a part of this incredible marathon experience. Whether you're running for fitness, competition, or simply for the joy of it, Oman Marathon Day has something for everyone.
